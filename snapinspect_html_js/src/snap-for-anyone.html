How SnapInspect Works for Everyone

SnapInspect's property inspection web app and mobile apps are applicable to a wide variety of industries. The web app makes it easy to manage teams, properties, work orders and inspections. Create your own checklist or customize an existing industry proven template or work with our support team to create the perfect template for your team. Use the mobile apps on iOS or Android to conduct inspections and record all essential data. Create a full comprehensive report with the push of a button. Customize reports based on industry standards or create your own visually unique report. Lastly put all your inspection data to work by utilising business intelligence to create insightful reports and generate interactive dashboards to help with inspection operations.

Customized Reports

Create your own reports or use one of our pre-made report templates. SnapInspect has the most powerful report tools in the market, we can replicate or create ANY report. Along with useful post inspection tools.

Schedule & Map Routes

Schedule inspections for the week and start them directly in the app without having to find the properties etc. Find map routes to all your properties in app and make sure your team and contractors never miss an inspection appointment.

Video Inspections

Conduct full video inspections or record video to capture sound and extra visuals to capture conditions. Video link will be embedded in the report and is hosted on our very own servers so rest assured video playback will always be quick and you won't be viewing any ads before playback.

Signatures

Record signatures after inspections to reduce risk and liability. Ensure all parties are on the same page and that contractors sign off accordingly. Gain signatures remotely via our advanced sharing features.

Manage Assets

Manage your rent roll/portfolio within our web app. Add any important information about each asset and property. Use our Business Intelligence to filter down and export asset info for data analysis or easy transfer into other applications.

Manage Reports

Generate inspection reports with a push of a button. Then edit it, convert it, merge it, split it, summarise it and email it all with a push of a button. Generate PDF and MS WORD reports. Manage every report inside the web app.

Integration

Sync your Google drive and Dropbox accounts to make sure that all your inspection data is automatically backed up in order of your properties. SnapInspect has an open API which means we can integrate with your management software and work order systems.

World Class Support

We reach out to you any way we can whether it be a phone call, email or in app message. Our support will ensure that we are with you every step of the way.
